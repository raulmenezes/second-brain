# History of AWS

Start up had to buy hardware
Previously cloud providers gave out contracts e.g. for 5 years
Had to be careful with the choses they made and paid for

Pay for the hour used

SQS - Messaging serve (2004)

AWS as a business (2006)

2007 - Went after the developers/ start-ups - 180,000 
E.g. Dropbox 

2010 - All of amazon.com moved over

2013 - Certification launched 

2017 - AI Services (powers Alexa)

Alexa Speciality Certification

**10,000 Foot Overview**

Availability Zone as a data center ( or 2 or more if close together)

Region geographical area

Edge locations are endpoints for AQS which are used for caching content. Consists of CloudFront, Amazon's CDN
More Edge locations than regions
