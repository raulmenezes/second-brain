# Side Project Ideas

## 1. Content & Social Media Tools

### Social Media Digest

Create a tool that:

- Aggregates posts from favorite:
    - Twitter/X accounts
    - Instagram accounts
    - Facebook pages
    - Subreddits
- Generates a **daily or weekly digest**
- Sends it automatically via **email or newsletter**

Potential features:
- AI summarization of posts
- Trending topic detection
- Custom categories (tech, food, finance, etc.)
---

### Quote → Image → Tweet Generator

Workflow tool that:
1. Generates a **quote**
2. Turns the quote into a **designed image**
3. Automatically posts to **Twitter/X**

Possible stack:
- AI text generation
- Image generation (templates or AI)
- Social media automation

---

## 2. Personal Automation Tools

### Wishlist Price Alert

A small automation service that:
- Monitors items on your **wishlist**
- Sends **SMS/text alerts** when prices drop

Possible integrations:
- Amazon
- eBay
- Shopify stores
- Price tracking APIs
