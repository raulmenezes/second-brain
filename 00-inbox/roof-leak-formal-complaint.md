# Roof Leak Formal Complaint

Prepared draft for a stage 1 formal complaint to FirstPort regarding the handling of a communal roof leak.

## Recipients

**To:** complaints@firstport.co.uk

**Cc:** Connor Underwood, Bethany Newton

## Draft

Dear FirstPort Complaints Team,

I am raising a formal complaint regarding the management of a communal roof leak affecting Flat 7, Fielding House, overseen by Property Manager Connor Underwood. Prolonged delays, lost documentation, and repeated administrative errors led to avoidable internal damage to my home, including carpet and mattress damage and water ingress through a light fitting.

## Timeline

- **26 Nov 2024:** Initial report submitted with photos and video evidence provided.
- **11 Dec 2024:** Acknowledged by Connor and referred to the claims team.
- **30 Jan 2025:** Contractor attended and identified **cracks on the roof**; photos were taken.
- **7 Feb 2025:** Connor stated he had received the job report and was awaiting a quote.
- **8–18 Feb 2025:** Multiple emails sent requesting a copy of the job report — **no response**.
- **5 Mar 2025:** Connor advised that the contractor did not complete a job card for the visit and that there were insufficient funds to proceed.
- **2 May 2025:** I requested an update — **no response received**.
- **9 May 2025:** I opened a **new case**, as Connor was not responding. He then stated that **no leak existed**, despite photographic evidence and prior inspection reports.
- **May–Jun 2025:** Several further emails exchanged urging Connor to instruct another contractor to inspect and resolve the issue.
- **20 Jun 2025:** Contractor **OnCall** finally attended and **reconfirmed the cracks** on the roof.
- **15 Aug 2025:** Repairs were finally completed.

## Requested Outcomes

- A written apology acknowledging the mishandling of the case.
- Confirmation that procedural changes will be made to prevent reports being lost or ignored.
- Reimbursement or replacement for damaged carpet and mattress, plus any related redecoration.
- Confirmation that any late payment penalties on the service charge will be removed.
