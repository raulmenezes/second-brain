# Thailand

Bangkok-focused trip planning note with places to visit, food spots, and a shortlist of higher-priority stops.

## Trip Snapshot

- Primary focus: Bangkok
- Mix of landmarks, restaurants, cafes, and shopping ideas
- Some items are aspirational and can be treated as optional overflow

## Top Priorities

- Wat Phra Kaew and the Grand Palace
- Wat Pho
- Wat Arun
- Talat Noi street art
- Chatuchak Weekend Market
- Wattana Panich
- Sam Lor
- Nhong Rim Klong
- Kho Panich
- Tichuca Rooftop Bar
